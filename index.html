About Me

I’m a passionate Web Design/UX student at Weber State University. My journey into tech started with a love for creativity and solving problems, which led me to design and development. I enjoy creating user-friendly, interactive web experiences that bring ideas to life. In my free time, I’m always eager to learn new skills and improve my craft.

Favorite Books

  • **The Pragmatic Programmer** - A must-read for any aspiring developer, offering practical advice on how to approach coding, problem-solving, and career development.
  • **Don't Make Me Think** - A simple yet powerful guide to user experience design, teaching how to create intuitive, user-friendly interfaces.
  • **Atomic Habits** - Great insights into building productive habits, something every student or professional should master.
  • **Clean Code** - A classic book on writing maintainable, efficient, and clean code, something I strive for in every project I work on.
